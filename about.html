The Journey

I am a Vietnamese-American who embarked on a new journey when I immigrated to the United States in late 2011. The following year, I settled in Garden Grove, CA, a community rich with Vietnamese-American culture, where my family and I found support despite our limited English proficiency.

My American education began at Jordan Intermediate and continued at Bolsa Grande High School. After graduating in 2018, I was accepted to California State University of Fullerton, where I majored in Computer Engineering. That August, alongside my college journey, I started my first job as a part-time barista at R&B Tea Garden Grove. Driven and committed, I was quickly promoted to Shift Leader after only five months. My tenure at R&B lasted nearly three years, even through the challenging COVID era.

In July 2021, I transitioned to Phuc Long Coffee & Tea as a Shift Manager, thanks to a friend's referral. Simultaneously, my academic endeavors were progressing. While completing my B.S. in Computer Engineering during my Junior year, I had the unique opportunity to lead Phuc Long Acoustic Band, later known as Mellow Official Band. I also served as a core member and project manager for Phuc Long Acoustic Contest Season 1.

Most recently, in May 2023, I graduated from California State University of Fullerton with Good Standing, earning my B.S. in Computer Engineering. My studies in Embedded Systems and FPGA have equipped me with valuable skills as I explore various opportunities to launch my career in the field. My journey so far is a testament to perseverance, adaptability, and an unending desire to learn and grow.
